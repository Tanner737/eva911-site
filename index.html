What makes EVA special

  • App‑optional alerts: Drivers can receive warnings in apps they already use (Waze). No mass app install campaign needed.
  • Fast to deploy: Truck‑mounted Android tablet + our cloud back‑end. No OEM car integrations required for pilots.
  • Smart geofencing: Moving, route‑aware geofences cut false alerts and focus only on affected drivers.
  • Privacy by design: We alert devices — we don’t track civilians. No personal location is stored.
  • Department‑friendly: Simple dispatch workflow (Send / Accept / Cancel) with audit logs and metrics.
  • Measurable impact: Capture seconds saved at key intersections and increased yielding rates.

How it works (60 seconds)

  1. Dispatch sends a route. The truck taps Accept.
  2. The truck streams GPS; EVA creates a moving alert zone ahead of it.
  3. Waze users in the zone see an on‑screen warning to yield.
  4. After pass‑by, alerts clear. Metrics log automatically.
